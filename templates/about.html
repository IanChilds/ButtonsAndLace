One day, over lunch in a cafe, a catch up turned into a conversation about starting a business together. After some very exciting chats with notepads and numerous cups of tea and bowls of chips, Buttons & Lace Photography was born! We are Megan and Rachel, two good friends with a passion for photography, who enjoy sparking off each other creatively as we work, and seeing things from different perspectives. Our style is natural, fun and creative, with a vintage twist – we love the details, and capturing special moments!

We enjoy taking natural, real photos – we wont be ‘in your face’, or shout at your guests; we are easygoing, relaxed and friendly, and want you to feel comfortable and happy being in front of our cameras!

Rachel and Megan
